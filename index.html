About Stoneworks

With over 20 years of experience, Stoneworks has built its name on premium workmanship, clean detailing and a refined approach to stone fabrication and installation.

We work mainly across Gauteng and Cape Town, while also taking on projects throughout South Africa and selected international work depending on scope.

Our work includes kitchens, bathrooms, vanities, basins, fireplaces, feature walls, custom stone elements, cut-to-size fabrication, polishing and finishing.

Where a project calls for a bold “solid-stone” appearance, we fabricate with carefully mitred edges and align the vein direction correctly to achieve a clean, high-end result with minimal visible joins.
