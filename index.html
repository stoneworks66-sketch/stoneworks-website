About Stoneworks

Stoneworks started in January 2004 with a focus on high-end stone craftsmanship, precision fabrication and exceptional installation quality.

We serve mainly Gauteng and Cape Town, while also taking on projects across South Africa and selected international work. From statement fireplaces and luxury bathrooms to bold kitchen islands and custom basins, our work is built to stand out.

We also offer trade services including cut-to-size, polishing, custom fabrication and specialist stone supply support.
